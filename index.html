Awareness for Food Waste Reduction


            

1. Meal Planning

Plan meals ahead and make shopping lists to avoid over-purchasing.

2. Proper Food Storage

Store food properly to extend its shelf life and prevent spoilage.

3. Creative Use of Leftovers

Use leftovers creatively in new meals or freeze them for later use.

4. Educational Campaigns

Educate through workshops and social media campaigns on food waste impacts.

5. Composting

Compost food scraps to reduce landfill waste and enrich the soil.

6. Food Donation

Promote food donation to local shelters or food banks to save surplus food.

7. Portion Control

Encourage proper portion control in households and restaurants to prevent waste.

8. Understanding Expiration Dates

Understand expiration labels (e.g., “best before” vs. “use by”) to avoid unnecessary discards.

9. Ugly Produce Sales

Support ugly produce sales in stores, reducing waste from cosmetic standards.

10. Policy Advocacy

Advocate for food waste policies that incentivize donation and reduce waste in businesses.
